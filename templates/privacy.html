Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account, we collect basic information such as your name and email address. As you use the Service, we also store the expense data you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information you provide to operate the Service — for example, to display your expense history, generate category breakdowns and monthly summaries, and let you sign in to your account. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely and retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time, and we will remove it within a reasonable timeframe.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting or email delivery services) to operate the Service. These providers only process data as necessary to provide their service to us and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on our website.
